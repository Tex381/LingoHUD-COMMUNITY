# Privacy Policy for LingoHUD

**Effective Date:** 2024-12-30

1. **Data Collection:** LingoHUD is a desktop application that processes data locally on your machine. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data, screen captures, or audio recordings on our servers.

2. **Third-Party Services:**
   - **Translation:** Text data is sent to the translation provider you select (e.g., Google Translate, DeepL) solely for the purpose of translation.
   - **Speech Recognition:** If enabled, audio data is processed locally (using Whisper) or sent to Google Speech API depending on your settings.

3. **Contact:** For any privacy concerns, please open an issue in this repository.
